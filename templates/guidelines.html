Mental Health Assessment Guidelines

Please ensure you read and understand these guidelines before starting the assessment. This will help us provide you with accurate and meaningful feedback.

  • This assessment is not a substitute for professional medical advice or therapy. For critical issues, please seek assistance from a licensed professional.
  • Your responses will remain private and confidential, and will only be used for assessment purposes.
  • Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability for accurate results.
  • Take the assessment in a quiet and distraction-free environment to focus on your thoughts and feelings.
  • The results are for informational purposes only and do not constitute a diagnosis.
  • Do not rush through the questions; take your time to reflect on each one.
  • If at any point you feel overwhelmed or distressed, you may exit the assessment and return when you feel ready.

By clicking "I Agree," you confirm that you have read and understood the guidelines and agree to proceed under these conditions.
